Joe "The Sniff" Newmar

From Accounting to Comedy

Joe "The Sniff" Newmar is a modern deadpan comedian who combines understated delivery, surreal observations, visual storytelling, and a healthy appreciation for life's absurdities.

Influenced by deadpan legends Steven Wright, Mitch Hedberg, and Demetri Martin, Newmar has developed a distinctive style that blends classic one-liners with props, AI-generated visuals, and creative storytelling. Working alongside his trusty "Prop Fairy," he transforms everyday situations into unexpected comedic experiences.

What makes Joe unique is his unlikely path to comedy. After a successful career in corporate accounting and executive leadership, he traded spreadsheets and SEC filings for stand-up comedy and filmmaking.

That journey became the foundation for his documentary:

From Accounting to Comedy, Sniff

A humorous and inspiring look at one man's unexpected pursuit of laughter, creativity, and reinvention.

The film combines stand-up comedy, documentary storytelling, visual humor, and a cast of memorable characters—including the legendary Prop Fairy.
